I am interested in Control, Optimization, and Machine Learning. In particular, I am interested in developing control algorithms to enhance robustness and athletic ability in two-legged dynamic systems for real-world applications.

Bio

Hochul Hwang was born in Seoul, Republic of Korea. He received his B.S. in Robot Engineering from Hanyang University at Ansan, Republic of Korea, in 2019. He conducted research as an intern at the Artificial Intelligence & Robotics Research Center at Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) from 2019 to 2020. Before joining KIST, he participated research as a research assistant in Human Centered Robotics Laboratory at the University of Texas at Austin. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst advised by Prof. Donghyun Kim.
