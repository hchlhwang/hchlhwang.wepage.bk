I am interested in Control, Optimization, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning. In particular, I am interested in developing control algorithms to enhance robustness, naturalness, and athletic ability in two-legged dynamic systems for real-world applications.

Bio

I was born in Seoul, Republic of Korea. I received my B.S. in Robotics Engineering from Hanyang University at Ansan, Republic of Korea, in 2019. I previously worked a research intern at the Artificial Intelligence & Robotics Research Center at Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) from 2019 to 2020. Before joining KIST, I participated research as a research assistant in Human Centered Robotics Laboratory at the University of Texas at Austin. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst fortunate to be advised by Prof. Donghyun Kim.
